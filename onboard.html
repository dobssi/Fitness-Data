👤 About You

❤️ Heart Rate

If you know your max HR and lactate threshold HR, enter them. Otherwise we'll estimate from your data.

▶ What's LTHR?
Lactate Threshold Heart Rate is roughly your average HR during a hard 30-minute effort or a 10K race. Your Garmin watch may report this — check Performance Stats → Lactate Threshold. If unsure, tick "Estimate" and we'll calculate it from your training data.

📂 Your Running Data

Upload your activity files and/or connect intervals.icu for automatic syncing.

▶ How to export from Garmin or Strava
Garmin Connect: Log in at connect.garmin.com → ⚙️ → Account Settings → Download Your Data → Export Your Data. You'll get an email with a zip file.

Strava: Log in at strava.com → Settings → My Account → Download or Delete Your Account → Request Your Archive. Takes a few hours.

🔗 Connect intervals.icu (optional — enables daily auto-sync)
▶ Where do I find these?
intervals.icu is a free training analysis platform that syncs with Garmin/Strava.

1. Create a free account at intervals.icu and connect your Garmin/Strava
2. Go to Settings → Developer
3. Your Athlete ID is shown at the top (e.g. i224884)
4. Click Generate API Key and copy it
